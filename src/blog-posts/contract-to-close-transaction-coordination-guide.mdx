# From Contract to Close: A Step-by-Step Guide to Real Estate Transaction Coordination

Transaction coordination is a crucial process in real estate. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Contract receipt and review
2. Opening escrow
3. Coordinating inspections and appraisals
4. Managing contingencies
5. Facilitating document signing
6. Coordinating with lenders and title companies
7. Final walkthrough arrangements
8. Closing preparation and attendance

Each step requires attention to detail and effective communication. A professional transaction coordinator ensures that every aspect of the process is handled efficiently and accurately.
